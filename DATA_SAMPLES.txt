1. COMPETITOR PRICING DATA
   File: raw/market/competitor_pricing.csv (972 rows)
   
   Sample Row:
   2023-01-01,KFC,Menu Item 21,32.83,35.66,−2.84,1.086
   
   Columns:
   - snapshot_date: Monthly snapshot (1st of each month, 12 snapshots)
   - competitor_name: KFC, Burger King, MOS Burger, Jollibee, Shake Shack
   - item_equivalent: Menu item being compared (from config MENU_ITEMS)
   - competitor_price_hkd: Price charged by competitor
   - abc_price_hkd: ABC Restaurant price for same/similar item
   - price_difference: Raw difference in HKD
   - price_index: Ratio (ABC Restaurant price / competitor price)
   
   Business Logic:
   - KFC typically 8% cheaper (price_multiplier: 0.92)
   - Burger King ~5% more expensive (1.05)
   - MOS Burger ~8% premium (1.08)
   - Jollibee 12% cheaper (0.88)
   - Shake Shack 40% premium (1.40)
   - Each item has ±5% random variance

   Use Case: Competitive positioning analysis, pricing strategy optimization

3. STORE P&L DATA

   2023-01-01,S001,752136.51,278572.14,473564.37,206903.69,159649,29264.85,22586.09,15887.65,39273.1,13943.04,25330.05,62.96,3.37
   
   Columns:
   - month: YYYY-MM-01

   - other_opex: Miscellaneous operating expenses
   - ebitda: Operating profit
   - depreciation: Equipment depreciation (~HK$8K-15K)
   - net_profit: Bottom line
   - gross_margin_pct: (Gross profit / revenue) × 100, ~60-68%
   - net_margin_pct: (Net profit / revenue) × 100, ~8-15%
   
   Revenue Drivers:
   - Base: store's avg_daily_orders × HK$55 avg_order_value × days_in_month
   - Seasonal multiplier: 1.08-1.15 (Jul-Aug, Dec), 0.95-1.05 (regular months)
   - Random variance: ±5% monthly fluctuation
   
   Use Case: Financial analysis, store profitability tracking, budget vs. actual

5. OPEX BREAKDOWN DATA
   File: raw/financial/opex_breakdown.csv (24,000 rows)
   
   Sample Row:
   2023-01-01,S001,labor,194438.26,179556.8,8.29
   
   Columns:
   - month: YYYY-MM-01
   - store_id: S001 to S200

   - supplies: HK$25K-50K (packaging, cleaning, etc.)
   - technology: HK$5K-15K (POS, systems)
   - training: HK$3K-12K
   - miscellaneous: HK$8K-20K
   
   Budget Variance:
   - Typical: ±10% variance (realistic operational reality)
   - Most stores within budget ±10%
   - Some outliers naturally occurring
   
   Use Case: Budget management, expense control, variance analysis

DATA GENERATION LOGIC SUMMARY
==============================================================================

REPRODUCIBILITY:
  - Random seed: 42
  - Identical output if regenerated with same seed
  - Useful for testing, development, demonstrations

REALISM FACTORS:
  1. Seasonal variation (summer tourism, winter holidays, typhoon season)
  2. Geographic variation (income, employment by district)
  3. Supply chain volatility (±5% variance, typhoon impacts)
  4. Budget variance (±10% realistic)
  5. Store type differences (mall stores higher labor costs due to rent)
  6. Competitor dynamics (realistic pricing relative to ABC Restaurant)

GEOGRAPHIC COVERAGE:
  - 18 HK districts with accurate population/economics
  - Tourist districts tracked separately
  - North-South income disparity modeled
  - Regional retail activity variations

TIME COVERAGE:
  - Full year 2023 (Jan 1 - Dec 31)
  - 12 monthly snapshots
  - Leap year not applicable (2023 is not leap year)
  - All holidays & seasons included in variance
